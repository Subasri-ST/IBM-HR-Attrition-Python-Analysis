# IBM HR Employee Attrition Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of 1,470 IBM employee records to understand 
why employees leave the company using Python.

## Tools & Skills Used
- Python
- Pandas (data manipulation)
- NumPy (numerical analysis)
- Matplotlib (data visualization)
- EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis)

## Key Findings
1. Overall attrition rate is 16.12% (237 out of 1,470)
2. Sales department has highest attrition at 20.63%
3. Young employees (18-25) leave most at 34.78%
4. Low salary employees leave 2x more than high earners
5. Overtime employees leave 3x more (30.53% vs 10.44%)
6. Sales Representative has highest attrition at 39.76%
7. Research Director has lowest attrition at only 2.50%
